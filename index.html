This start up aims to make young people travel by making them discover new cultures and languages while practicing their passion, boxing. There are many trips, today three are available. These trips are in the name of the great boxers of the region where the youngsters will stay.

Our Trips

Krongsak Prakong

trip to Bangkok, Thailand which is the cradle of Thai boxing also known as Kicksboxing.

Muhammad Ali

trip to Los Angeles, the United States. This city is not Kentucky but it is the city of the show, where many fights took place.

Tyson Fury

trip to London, England.This city is not his birthplace but is the capital of English and international boxing.
